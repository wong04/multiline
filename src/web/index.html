How to play 🪐

Goal

Get your planets in a row before your opponent — across, down, diagonally within one board, or stepping diagonally across neighbouring timelines.

Your turn

Click an empty cell to drop your planet. Players alternate.

Forking timelines 🔀

Turn on Fork, then click an empty cell to spin off a new timeline — a copy of that board. Copied planets appear as faded echoes; ones you place are bright. You can fork up to the timeline limit of the ruleset.

Winning across timelines ✨

A winning row can step from one board into the timeline next to it. You need as many timelines as your target row length (e.g. 4-in-a-row needs 4 boards in a diagonal).

Rulesets

  • Classic — one board, 5 in a row. Just the basics.
  • Branching — small board, 3 in a row, up to 3 timelines. Easy forks.
  • Full — 8×8, 4 in a row, up to 4 timelines. The real game.

Modes

🤖 vs Computer (easy/normal/hard) · 🛋 Hotseat (same device) · 🌍 Online (share a code) · 🛰 Quick-match (random opponent).
