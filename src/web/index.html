How to play 🪐

Goal

Get your planets in a row before your opponent — across, down, diagonally within one board, or stepping diagonally across neighbouring timelines.

Your turn

Click an empty cell to drop your planet. Players alternate.

Forking timelines 🔀

Turn on Fork, then click an empty cell to spin off a new timeline — a copy of that board (both players' planets carry over as faded echoes). It opens a whole new board your opponent must also defend. You can fork up to the timeline limit of the ruleset.

Winning across timelines ✨

A winning row can step diagonally from one board into the timeline next to it — and a cross-timeline row needs fewer planets than an in-board one (e.g. 5 in a board, but just 3 across timelines). That's the fast lane to victory.

Why fork? 🤔

A cross-timeline row needs fewer planets than an in-board one, and every fork adds a board your opponent must also defend. Forking stretches them thin across timelines — build a diagonal they can't cover everywhere at once.

Rulesets

  • Classic — one board, 5 in a row. Just the basics, no forking.
  • Branching — 6×6, 4 in a board or 3 across timelines, up to 3 timelines. Learn to fork.
  • Full — 10×10, 5 in a board or 3 across timelines, up to 4 timelines. The real game.

Modes

🤖 vs Computer (easy/normal/hard) · 🛋 Hotseat (same device) · 🌍 Online (share a code) · 🛰 Quick-match (random opponent).
